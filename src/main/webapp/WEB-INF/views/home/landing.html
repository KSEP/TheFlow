About theFlow

theFlow is the smart and simple issue tracking system. It will help you to provide your project management on a new level. With theFlow you are always aware of your tasks status. Task distribution is seen to every member of your team, this helps to make task distribution more automatically and to avoid synchronous execution of the same work by team members. theFlow will save your time and increase work productivity. You can try it with a simple registration or with built in credentials, which you can find on the login page. Your feedbacks or proposals are very important to us, so you can leave your comment at the bottom.

Please attention! This is the demo version deployed for demonstration and testing. Database refreshes time-to-time, your data can be removed.

Technologies

theFlow is being developing with use of up-to-date technologies. We use Spring, Spring Security, Hibernate, MySql, Thymeleaf, Grunt, Bower. Now you can see the short version of the project. Many modules and features are going to be added, or are in work just now, such as time management and agile tools.
